# 📊 Regional Sales Analysis Dashboard

An end-to-end Data Analytics & Business Intelligence project focused on analyzing regional sales performance across the United States using Python, Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), and Power BI.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Sales teams often struggle to identify:
- High-performing regions
- Profitable sales channels
- Seasonal revenue trends
- Top-performing products & customers

This project analyzes **5 years of historical sales data** to uncover actionable business insights and support strategic decision-making through interactive dashboards and visual analytics.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

The organization faced:
- Inconsistent revenue and profit across regions
- Limited visibility into seasonal trends
- Difficulty identifying profitable products & channels
- Lack of centralized reporting for business users

### Objective
Build a data-driven solution to:
- Analyze sales performance
- Identify growth opportunities
- Track profitability
- Enable self-service business reporting
